Prawns Fry

Home

Description


Prawns fry is a delicious seafood dish made by marinating prawns with spices and shallow frying them until golden and crispy

Its flavorful, easy to prepare, and often served as a starter or side dish.



Ingredients

  • 500g prawns, cleaned and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons rice flour (optional, for extra crispiness)
  • Oil for shallow frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, lemon juice, and salt to create a marinade.
  2. Add the cleaned prawns to the marinade and let them sit for at least 30 minutes.
  3. If using, sprinkle rice flour over the marinated prawns for extra crispiness.
  4. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Once the oil is hot, add the marinated prawns in batches, ensuring not to overcrowd the pan.
  6. Fry the prawns for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until they turn golden brown and crispy.
  7. Remove the prawns from the pan and drain them on paper towels to remove excess oil.
  8. Serve hot with lemon wedges and your choice of dipping sauce.
